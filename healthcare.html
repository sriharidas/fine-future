Healthcare

What is Healthcare?

Healthcare is the method of maintenance or improvement of health by treatment, cure or recovery. Healthcare services are delivered by the expertise in medical field, nurse, dentist, physician and other professionals who are related to health and medical departments.

Role of healthcare

Healthcare has become one of the largest sectors in both revenue and employment. Healthcare comprises hospitals, clinics, health insurance and medical equipment. The healthcare sector is growing at a high rate due to its strengthening coverage, services and increasing expenditure.

Categories

Healthcare delivery system is categorized into two major components - public and private.

  • The private sector provides majority of secondary, tertiary, and quaternary care institutions with major concentration in metros.
  • The public healthcare system comprises limited secondary and tertiary care institutions in key cities and focuses on providing basic healthcare facilities in the form of primary healthcare centers (PHC) in rural areas.

Levels of healthcare

Our healthcare services are classified into 3 levels as folowing.

  • Primary Healthcare Service
  • Secondary Healthcare Service
  • Tertiary Healthcare Service

The difference between primary, Secondary and tertiary healthcare system are,

  • the primary healthcare system mainly focuses on prevention for diseases.
  • whereas, Secondary and Tertiary Systems deals with curing of diseases.

Primary Healthcare Service

In this primary healthcare system first patient consults doctors or physician directly and healthcare services are provided as a prevention stage.

Secondary Healthcare Service

Suppose if primary healthcare unable to prevent disease, they will send the patient to secondary healthcare of curing. They will provide basic curative for the patients.

Tertiary Healthcare Service

When there is lack of facilities in secondary healthcare, patient will be sent to tertiary healthcare. Tertiary healthcare will cure the disease with advanced facilities.
